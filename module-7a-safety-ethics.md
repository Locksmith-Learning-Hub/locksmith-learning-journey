# ⚠️ Module 7: Safety & Ethics

## 📌 Overview
This module focuses on the legal, ethical, and professional responsibilities of a locksmith. Beyond technical skills, trust, safety, and correct judgment are critical in this field. A locksmith is often given access to private property, making ethical conduct essential.

---

## ⚖️ Legal Considerations (Authorized Access Only)

Locksmith work must always be performed within legal boundaries.

### 🔍 Key Principles:
- Only provide access with proper authorization  
- Always verify ownership or permission before proceeding  
- Refuse service if the situation appears suspicious  
- Follow local laws and regulations  

> ⚠️ Unauthorized entry is illegal and can have serious consequences.

---

## 🔓 Importance of Non-Destructive Techniques

Professional locksmiths prioritize methods that avoid damage.

### 💡 Why It Matters:
- Preserves the lock and door  
- Reduces cost for the customer  
- Maintains professional standards  
- Minimizes risk during the job  

### 🧠 Best Practice:
- Always attempt non-destructive entry first  
- Use destructive methods only when necessary  

> 💡 A skilled locksmith solves problems with minimal impact.

---

## 🛡️ Customer Verification and Trust

Trust is one of the most important aspects of this profession.

### 🔐 Verification Process:
- Ask for identification when needed  
- Confirm that the person has the right to access the property  
- Be cautious in unclear situations  

### 🤝 Building Trust:
- Be transparent about the process  
- Explain what you are doing  
- Provide honest advice and solutions  

> 💡 Trust once lost is difficult to regain — always act professionally.

---

## ⚠️ Professional Ethics

Ethical behavior defines a reliable locksmith.

### ✔️ Key Values:
- Honesty in pricing and work  
- Respect for customer privacy  
- Responsibility in handling tools and access  
- Commitment to quality work  

### ❌ What to Avoid:
- Misleading customers  
- Unnecessary damage or upselling  
- Ignoring safety procedures  

---

## 🚫 Risk Awareness

Locksmith work involves responsibility and potential risks.

### ⚠️ Examples:
- Gaining access to sensitive areas  
- Handling emergency situations  
- Working under time pressure  

👉 Good judgment is essential in every situation.

---

## 🎯 Key Learning Outcomes

By completing this module, you should be able to:

- Understand the legal boundaries of locksmith work  
- Apply ethical decision-making in real situations  
- Recognize the importance of customer trust  
- Prioritize safety and responsibility  

---

## 💡 Practical Insight

- Technical skills alone are not enough  
- Professional behavior builds long-term success  
- Customers value trust and honesty as much as skill
