# 🔐 Locksmith Learning Journey  
### Self-Learning Outline – Beginner to Intermediate

👉 📘 **Start Here: [Course Navigation (Module 0)](./module-0-course-navigation.md)**

This repository documents my structured self-learning journey into the field of locksmithing.  
The focus is on developing a clear understanding of lock mechanisms, practical workflows, and real-world problem-solving required in a professional locksmith environment.

---

## 🎯 Objective
To build a strong foundation in locksmith fundamentals through self-learning, preparing for hands-on training and real field experience.

---

## 🧠 Module 1: Lock Fundamentals
- Types of locks:
  - Cylinder locks (Euro, mortice)
  - Deadbolts
  - Padlocks
- Basic lock anatomy:
  - Pins, springs, plug, shear line
- Understanding how locking mechanisms work

---

## 🔓 Module 2: Non-Destructive Entry (Concept Level)
- Lock picking basics (theory only)
- Tools overview:
  - Picks
  - Tension wrench
- Raking vs single pin picking (concept)
- Identifying when non-destructive entry is possible

---

## 🔧 Module 3: Lock Replacement & Installation
- Removing and replacing euro cylinders
- Measuring cylinder sizes accurately
- Door types:
  - Wooden doors
  - uPVC basics
- Handle alignment and lock fitting basics

---

## 🚪 Module 4: Common Field Jobs
- Home lockouts (approach and safety awareness)
- Broken key scenarios (basic understanding)
- Troubleshooting jammed locks
- Customer interaction during service calls

---

## 🚗 Module 5: Automotive Basics (Intro Only)
- Car lockout concepts
- Types of car keys:
  - Mechanical
  - Remote / transponder (basic awareness)

---

## 🛠️ Module 6: Tools Familiarisation
- Lock pick sets (overview)
- Air wedge and entry tools
- Basic hand tools:
  - Screwdrivers
  - Drills
- Safe handling of tools

---

## ⚠️ Module 7: Safety & Ethics
- Legal considerations (authorized access only)
- Importance of non-destructive techniques
- Customer verification and trust

---

## 💬 Module 8: Customer Handling
- Calm and professional communication
- Explaining issues in simple terms
- Handling emergency situations effectively

---

## 🚀 Learning Approach
- Concept-first understanding  
- Focus on real-world application  
- Emphasis on safety, ethics, and customer trust  
- Continuous improvement through observation and practice  

---

## 📌 Note
This repository reflects a learning foundation and conceptual understanding of locksmithing.  
The next step is to apply these concepts in practical, hands-on training within a professional environment.

---

## 🤝 Goal
To transition into a professional locksmith role, develop advanced skills, and provide reliable, high-quality service.
