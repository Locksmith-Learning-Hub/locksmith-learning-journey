# 🚪 Module 4: Common Field Jobs

## 📌 Overview
This module introduces real-world locksmith situations that are commonly encountered during field work. It focuses on understanding practical scenarios, problem-solving approaches, and maintaining safety and professionalism while working on-site.

---

## 🔓 Home Lockouts (Approach & Safety Awareness)

Home lockouts are one of the most common locksmith jobs.

### 🧠 Key Considerations:
- Verify customer identity before proceeding  
- Assess the type and condition of the lock  
- Choose the least invasive method first  
- Prioritize non-destructive entry whenever possible  

### ⚠️ Safety Awareness:
- Ensure no risk to the customer or property  
- Avoid unnecessary damage to doors or locks  
- Maintain a calm and professional approach  

> 💡 A successful locksmith balances speed, safety, and minimal damage.

---

## 🔑 Broken Key Scenarios (Basic Understanding)

Broken keys inside locks are a frequent issue.

### 🔍 Common Causes:
- Worn or weak keys  
- Excessive force during use  
- Internal lock resistance  

### 🧠 Basic Approach:
- Assess how much of the key is inside the lock  
- Determine whether removal is possible without damage  
- Avoid pushing the broken piece further inside  

> ⚠️ Improper handling can worsen the situation and damage the lock.

---

## 🔧 Troubleshooting Jammed Locks

Jammed locks require careful diagnosis before action.

### 🔍 Possible Causes:
- Misalignment of the lock or door  
- Dirt or debris inside the mechanism  
- Worn internal components  

### 🧠 Problem-Solving Approach:
- Inspect external alignment first  
- Test the key movement gently  
- Identify whether the issue is mechanical or alignment-related  

> 💡 Understanding the cause is more important than applying force.

---

## 💬 Customer Interaction During Service Calls

Customer communication is a critical part of locksmith work.

### 🤝 Key Skills:
- Listen carefully to the customer's issue  
- Explain the situation in simple terms  
- Provide clear and honest solutions  
- Maintain a calm and respectful attitude  

### 🧠 Professional Behavior:
- Be punctual and reliable  
- Respect customer property  
- Build trust through transparency  

> 💡 A professional attitude often matters as much as technical skill.

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Skipping identity verification  
- Using force without proper assessment  
- Misdiagnosing the problem  
- Poor communication with the customer
