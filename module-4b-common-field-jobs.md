Home lockouts are one of the most common locksmith jobs.

### 🧠 Key Considerations:

* Verify customer identity before proceeding
* Assess the type and condition of the lock
* Choose the least invasive method first
* Prioritize non-destructive entry whenever possible

### ⚠️ Safety Awareness:

* Ensure no risk to the customer or property
* Avoid unnecessary damage
* Stay calm and professional

👉 A good locksmith balances speed, safety, and minimal damage.

Broken keys inside locks are a frequent issue.

### 🔍 Common Causes:

* Worn or weak keys
* Excessive force during use
* Internal lock resistance

### 🧠 Basic Approach:

* Check how much of the key is inside
* Decide if it can be removed safely
* Avoid pushing the broken part deeper

👉 Careful handling prevents further damage.

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

* Skipping identity verification
* Using force without checking the problem
* Misdiagnosing the issue
* Poor communication with customers

## 🎯 Key Learning Points

* Field jobs require quick but careful decisions
* Safety and professionalism are essential
* Non-destructive methods should be prioritized
* Customer handling is as important as technical skill
