### 🔧 Basic Process:

1. Locate the fixing screw on the edge of the door
2. Remove the screw using a screwdriver
3. Insert the key and slightly turn it to align the cam
4. Gently pull the cylinder out

### 🔁 Replacement:

* Insert the new cylinder
* Align it correctly
* Tighten the fixing screw
* Test the lock multiple times

👉 Always ensure smooth operation before finishing the job
