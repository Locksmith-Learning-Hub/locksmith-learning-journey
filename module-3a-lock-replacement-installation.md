# 🔧 Module 3: Lock Replacement & Installation

## 📌 Overview
This module introduces the practical side of locksmithing, focusing on how locks are safely removed, measured, and installed. These are essential skills used in everyday locksmith work, especially in residential and commercial environments.

---

## 🔄 Removing and Replacing Euro Cylinders

Euro cylinders are one of the most common lock types used in modern doors, especially uPVC systems.

### 🔧 Basic Process:
1. Identify the fixing screw on the edge of the door  
2. Remove the screw using a screwdriver  
3. Insert the key and slightly turn it to align the cam  
4. Gently slide the cylinder out of the door  

### 🔁 Replacement:
- Insert the new cylinder in the same position  
- Align the cam properly  
- Secure it with the fixing screw  
- Test the lock using the key  

> 💡 Tip: Always test the lock multiple times after installation to ensure smooth operation.

---

## 📏 Measuring Cylinder Sizes Accurately

Correct measurement is critical to ensure proper fit and security.

### 📐 Measurement Method:
- Measure from the center of the fixing hole (screw position)  
- Measure both sides:
  - Inside length  
  - Outside length  

### Example:
- 30mm / 40mm (inside / outside)

> ⚠️ Incorrect sizing can lead to security risks or improper fitting.

## 🚪 Door Types

Understanding different door types helps determine the correct installation approach.

### 🪵 Wooden Doors
- Typically use mortice locks  
- Require internal fitting داخل door body  
- May involve more manual alignment and cutting  

---

### 🚪 uPVC Doors (Basics)
- Common in modern homes  
- Use multi-point locking systems  
- Euro cylinders are standard  
- Easier to replace compared to traditional locks  

---

## 🔩 Handle Alignment and Lock Fitting Basics

Proper alignment ensures smooth operation and long-term durability.

### Key Points:
- Ensure handles are level and properly fixed  
- Check that the latch and bolt align with the strike plate  
- Avoid excessive force when fitting components  
- Tighten screws securely but do not overtighten  

> 💡 Poor alignment can cause lock failure or difficulty in operation.

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Installing the wrong cylinder size  
- Misaligning the lock mechanism  
- Overtightening screws  
- Not testing the lock after installation
