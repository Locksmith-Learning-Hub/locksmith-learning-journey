Car lockouts happen when access to a vehicle is lost.

### 🔍 Common Scenarios:

* Keys locked inside the vehicle
* Lost or misplaced keys
* Faulty central locking system
* Remote key not working

### 🧠 Basic Approach:

* Identify the type of vehicle and lock system
* Choose safe entry points
* Use non-destructive techniques
* Avoid damage to the vehicle

👉 Modern vehicles require careful handling due to electronics.
