# 🔓 Module 2: Non-Destructive Entry (Concept Level)

## 📌 Overview

Non-destructive entry (NDE) means opening a lock **without damaging it**.
A professional locksmith always tries this method first to keep the lock usable and avoid extra cost for the customer.

Basic idea:

* Locks contain pins and springs inside
* A key aligns the pins to a correct height
* When pins align at the shear line → the lock opens

👉 Lock picking simulates this process manually.

## 🔍 When to Use Non-Destructive Entry

### ✅ Suitable situations:

* Lock is in good condition
* Key is lost but lock is not damaged
* Customer wants to avoid replacement

---

### ❌ Not suitable when:

* Lock is broken internally
* Key is stuck or damaged inside
* High-security or anti-pick locks are used

---

## ⚠️ Safety and Professional Ethics

* Always work with proper authorization
* Avoid unnecessary damage
* Respect customer property
* Maintain professionalism and trust

---

## 🎯 Key Learning Points

* Non-destructive entry is the first approach
* Understanding lock behavior is essential
* Tools require control and precision
* Not all locks can be opened this way
